品牌印记

结合行业属性以及业务场景，以生物医药通用符号「+」作为基础，45度倾斜后变形为 x ，符号重复后构成 「DNA」的意像，传达品牌无限可能性和蓬勃向上的力量。

 

Combining industry attributes and business scenarios, the universal biomedical symbol "+" is used as the basis, which is transformed into an x ​​after being tilted 45 degrees. The symbol is repeated to form the image of "DNA", conveying the brand's infinite possibilities and vigorous power.
